SEO & SMO Strategies

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Implemented SEO Strategies:

It happens that most SEO strategies work in the real world. One such example is before and after joining Nike, which increased the visibility of the website by optimizing it for use by voice assistants as the number of consumers continues rising with their voice assistants. Nike also redesigned its product descriptions and meta content to mimic human conversational queries. The increase from voice search by 30 per cent was seen suddenly reflected in online sales. This has shown the real business impact that can be made possible with the merging of SEO and technologies like voice search (PageTraffic, 2024).

An equally persuasive example of image-based SEO in user experience optimization would include its use by Sephora. Sephora has now set a page-search function, where users could upload images of the looks they want to achieve with the makeup. It can work on optimized image tags and structured data to draw relationships between uploaded images to relevant suggestions of products. Hence, Sephora has experienced a 15% increase in the conversion rate with brand engagement. These outcomes show how well aligned SEO with more innovative UX features can capture traffic and conversions. This is supported by PageTraffic (2024) and SEMrush (2024).

Among the most recent models of SEO success come out of Moz's strategic plan to optimize quality, user-focused content. Moz produced long guides and blog posts answering very specific questions that users generally asked about SEO tools and techniques. Optimized for targeted long-tail keywords, structured for featured snippets, and employing any other optimization technique, Moz thereafter succeeded in regularly attaining a "position zero" in search engine results. The approach yielded a 20% annual increase in organic traffic to the firm while at the same time solidified its leading stature on the SEO sector. It sends the success signals of combining useful information with keywords and snippets.
